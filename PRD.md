# Student-OS Product Requirements Document

## Vision

Student-OS is an AI-powered academic workspace that helps students manage their courses, assignments, study sessions, and schedules from one place.

Our goal is to reduce the number of apps students need to use every day.

---

# Problem

Students constantly switch between:

- Canvas
- Google Calendar
- Notion
- ChatGPT
- Quizlet
- Notes
- To-do lists

Everything is scattered.

Student-OS brings everything together.

---

# Target Users

- High School Students
- College Students
- Master's Students

---

# Version 1 Features

## Authentication

- Login
- Sign Up
- Forgot Password

---

## Onboarding

- Name
- Student Type
- College (Optional)
- Major (Optional)
- Semester (Optional)

---

## Courses

Users can create courses.

Each course can optionally include a syllabus PDF.

The AI automatically extracts:

- Weekly topics
- Exams
- Assignments
- Important dates

Users can edit everything afterward.

---

## Dashboard

Displays:

- Today's study sessions
- Upcoming assignments
- Calendar
- AI recommendations

---

## Study Mode

- Pomodoro timer
- Progress tracking
- Resume where you left off

---

## Calendar

Users can add:

- Classes
- Exams
- Assignments
- Personal events

---

## AI Planner

Creates study schedules based on deadlines.
